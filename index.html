About Us

About Company

ARETE Consulting Engineers

ARETE CONSULTING ENGINEERS is a Multi-Disciplinary Engineering Consultancy company founded in 2013 to cater the needs of ever changing construction industry with the current consultancy services available with the objective to provide innovative and sustainable solutions to recreate the built environment.


Arete has earned reputation in providing Quality Engineering Solutions with its constant and dedicated pursuit for technical excellence in the field of HVAC, Refrigeration, Building Management system and Green building.


Arete has executed wide range of projects of various applications such as Hotels, Corporate offices, Prestigious show rooms, Software Development Centers, Clubs/Resorts, Laboratories and clean rooms, Hospitals, Manufacturing/Industries, Auditoriums/Multiplexes, Malls, Apartments, Residentials towers, Jewellary, Cold Stores etc.,
